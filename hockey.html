Sidney Patrick Crosby ONS is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who serves as captain of the Pittsburgh Penguins of the National Hockey League

Jonathan Bryan Toews OM is a Canadian professional ice hockey centre who currently serves as captain of the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League. Toews was selected by the Blackhawks with the third overall pick in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft

Connor McDavid is a Canadian professional ice hockey centre and captain for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League

Stanley Mikita was a Slovak-born Canadian professional ice hockey player for the Chicago Black Hawks of the National Hockey League, generally regarded as the best centre of the 1960s. In 2017, he was named one of the 100 Greatest NHL Players.

Carey Price is a Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender who plays for the Montreal Canadiens of the National Hockey League

Gordon Howe OC was a Canadian professional ice hockey player. From 1946 to 1980, he played twenty-six seasons in the National Hockey League and six seasons in the World Hockey Association; his first 25 seasons were spent with the Detroit Red Wings

Alexander Mikhailovich Ovechkin, often referred to as "the Great Eight" or "Ovi", is a Russian professional ice hockey winger and captain of the Washington Capitals of the National Hockey League. He is regarded as one of the greatest hockey players in the history of the NHL.

 

 

Pernell-Karl Sylvester "P. K." Subban MSC is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman for the Nashville Predators of the National Hockey League. Subban was drafted by the Montreal Canadiens in the 2nd round, 43rd overall, of the 2007 NHL Entry Draft.

Patrick Timothy Kane II, is an American professional ice hockey right winger for the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League. The Blackhawks selected him with the first overall pick in the 2007 NHL Entry Draft. Kane also represented the United States at the 2010 and 2014 Winter Olympics.
